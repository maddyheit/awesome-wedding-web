Our Story

Not so long ago, Maddy and Jesse were on opposite coasts.

And now there will be two weddings with two champagne toasts.

  • Our First Date

    June 4, 2016

    Jesse picked up Maddy and took her to American Tandoor. He ordered the lamb vindaloo, she ordered the butter chicken and they both enjoyed the decor. They shared their dinners with each other and each had a beer. When Jesse took Maddy back, he offered her his leftovers as a souvenir.

  • We Moved in Together

    November 5th, 2017

    Maddy had been offically living on the East Coast for 2 years, so they sat down and talked about moving in together over beers. They loved each other enough to give it a test, they decideed to take a chance together and were happy to finally build their own nest.

  • We Got Engaged

    March 17, 2018

    Maddy wanted to go to Baltimore to get brunch and see the aquarium on her birthday, she didn't understand why Jesse wanted to leave so early on the beltway. He took her to Federal Hill Park early like he had planned, to get on one knee, ask her to marry him and put her engagement ring on her hand.
